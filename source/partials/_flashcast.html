Flashcast

Discord flashcard app

Five years ago I searched for a specific flashcard app feature that did not exist. So, now that I can code I made it myself! This app uses discord to send messages to users at a specific timeframe (set by the user). The app just sends you a card to answer. You don't have to remember to open an app, you don't get bogged down with dozens of due cards, this app makes it super simple. I love it and have been using it constantly since I made it.
